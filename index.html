About

This website is designed to provide an oppurtunity to students to show their technical caliber to the companies and get themselves hired. All the companies show their requirement along with tentative number of openings. students has to register themselves for that particular examination. The test will be based on the technical requirement of the company. There will be total three layers of selection. First layer will be prelimenary test. Students selected in first layer need to apply for the second test, which will be main test. After that the students will be called for the interview at the company campus.

Note: It should be noted that all the openings are as per specific requirements, hence tests will be conducted as per that specific requirement.
